help1004||4047||your purchase history|| how to delete items in your purchase history while you can,t permanently delete items from your purchase history, you can hide the items you,ve bought in the last 60 days. select the more actions dropdown on the item you,d like to remove, and choose hide item . if you,ve hidden something by mistake, simply select undo . tip when you,re in the more actions menu, you can also contact the seller, start a return, leave feedback or even start selling yourself.
