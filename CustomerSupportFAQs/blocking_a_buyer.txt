blocking a buyer
help1034||4082||blocking a buyer|| to stop a particular member from being able to bid on or buy your items: go to the block bidders or buyers from your listings page. enter their username. (you can add up to 5,000 usernames) select submit . tip you can restore a past blocked bidder/buyer list by selecting on restore list . if you want to unblock someone at a later date: go to the block bidders or buyers from your listings page. delete the username of the member from the text box. select submit . you can also choose to block buyers based on them not meeting specific requirements, such as a positive feedback score, or living in a country that you are willing to post items to. here,s how: go to selling preferences in my ebay. select show next to buyer requirements. beside block buyers who , choose edit . enter your preferences and select submit .
