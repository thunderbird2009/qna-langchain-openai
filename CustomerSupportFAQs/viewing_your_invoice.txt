viewing your invoice
help1095||4126||viewing your invoice|| here,s how to view your ebay invoice: sign in to your account. hover over your username at the top of the page and select account settings . from the invoices dropdown menu, select the monthly invoice you,re looking for, then select go . your invoice will appear in a new window. from your invoice, you,ll see links to print or download. see your invoice if you have a question about one of the charges, go to your account activity , scroll to the bottom of the page, and select the tab for the details you,d like to see: fees , credits , payments &amp; refunds , or promotional savings . tip fees or credits added since your invoice was created won,t show up until the following month.
