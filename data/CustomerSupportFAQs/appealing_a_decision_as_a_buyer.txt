help1308||4039||appealing a decision as a buyer|| need selling help? read our article on appealing a decision as a seller here,s how to appeal a decision: find the item in your purchase history , and select see case details . select send us an appeal . fill in the details and select submit . you,ll need to provide new information in your appeal, such as: photos showing that the item doesn,t match the listing description tracking details showing that the seller received the item you returned proof that your item was delivered to the wrong address a copy of a police report if applicable we,ll review the new information and get back to you with a final decision. this usually takes up to 48 hours, although it can be a little longer in some circumstances. tip make sure to start your appeal within 30 days of the case being closed.
